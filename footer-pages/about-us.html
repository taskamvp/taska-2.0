About Taska

Empowering college students with practical learning through part-time work opportunities

Our Story

Founded in 2025 by passionate students from IIT Delhi, Taska was born from a simple yet powerful vision: to bridge the gap between academic learning and real-world experience. We recognized that college students across India have incredible potential but often lack opportunities to apply their knowledge in practical settings.

Our mission is to create a dynamic workplace environment where college students can work part-time, gain valuable experience, and develop essential skills while pursuing their education. We believe that practical learning is the key to preparing students for successful careers.

Our Mission

To provide college students with meaningful part-time work opportunities that complement their academic learning, enabling them to gain practical experience and develop professional skills.

Our Vision

To become India's leading platform for student employment, creating a bridge between education and industry while empowering the next generation of professionals.
